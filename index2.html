VIVID SYDNEY

这款移动应用程序旨在通过创新的数字解决方案提升缤纷悉尼灯光音乐节的体验。用户可以通过在应用程序中设置偏好来设计个性化行程。系统会创建一个初始计划，用户可以根据自身需求添加或删除活动进行优化。该应用程序还会在交互式地图上显示行程路线，提供直观的导航和清晰的视觉指南。

The mobile app aims to enhance the Vivid Sydney festival experience through innovative digital solutions. Users can design personalized itineraries by setting their preferences within the app. The system then creates an initial plan, which users can refine by adding or removing activities to suit their needs. The app also displays the itinerary route on an interactive map, offering straightforward navigation and a clear visual guide.
