VIVID SYDNEY MAP MIMA 光速咖啡 南阳光屿 LUCKY VITALITY

该项目是设计一个虚构原型：如果在未来的日常生活中保健品以“盲盒”形式销售会怎样？在不久的将来，BLND.lab已发展成为全球领先的盲盒文化品牌，产品线涵盖玩具、美妆、食品、时尚和家居用品。其最受关注的创新产品之一是Lucky Vitality——一个将自我护理重新定义为机会游戏的盲盒健康系列。这些时尚未来主义的扭蛋机被精心布置在各大城市最繁忙的街角和交通枢纽。

The project was to design a fictional prototype: what would happen if health products were sold in the form of “blind boxes” in our daily lives in the future? In the near future, BLND.lab has grown into a leading global brand in blind box culture, with product lines spanning toys, beauty, food, fashion, and home goods. One of its most talked-about innovations is Lucky Vitality, a blind box health series that redefines self-care as a game of chance.These sleek, futuristic gacha machines are strategically placed at the busiest street corners and transit hubs across major cities.

设计方案 | Design Scheme

手册 | Manual Sheet

当用户在线购买幸运活力产品时，他们将收到这本小册子，其中介绍了当前季度的所有类别以及下一季度即将推出的产品。

When users purchase lucky vitality online, they will receive this Manual Sheet, which introduces all the categories of the current season and the products to be launched in the next quarter.

图解指南 | Illustrated Guide

产品罐子外包装设计过程中我运用AIGC辅助设计，生成符合主题的个性化插画设计，同时确保同一主题下不同产品设计的一致性。

During the design process of the product can packaging, I used AIGC to assist in designing, generating personalized illustrations that matched the theme while ensuring consistency in the designs of different products under the same theme.
