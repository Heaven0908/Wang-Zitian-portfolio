VIVID SYDNEY MAP MIMA 光速咖啡 南阳光屿 LUCKY VITALITY

该项目是为南阳光屿产业社区做品牌设计，包括Logo设计，IP形象设计以及其衍生物料。南阳光屿社区以 “阳光” 为纽带，以 “小岛” 为空间载体，打造艺术策展型社区场域。在这里，阳光与岛民的生活交织共振，激发充满想象的社区化学反应。

This project involves branding for the Sun Island Industrial Community, including logo design, IP identity design, and related materials. Using "sunlight" as a connection and the "island" as a spatial medium, the Nanyangguang Island community creates an art-curated community space. Here, sunlight intertwines and resonates with the lives of islanders, sparking a vibrant and imaginative community chemistry.
