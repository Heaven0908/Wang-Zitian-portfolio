光速咖啡

该项目是为杭州生物科技谷的咖啡店做品牌设计，包括LOGO设计，店招设计以及品牌物料衍生。杭州生物科技谷位于杭州市临空经济示范区内，围绕创新药、CDMO、“高轻新”型医疗器械、高端美妆用品，打造位列浙江省第一梯队兼具国内外影响力的生物医药产业集群。该咖啡品牌总体思路需要体现产业园区、科技感与咖啡的关联性。

This project involved branding for a coffee shop in Hangzhou Biotech Valley, including logo design, signage, and branding materials. Located within the Hangzhou Airport Economic Demonstration Zone, Hangzhou Biotech Valley is developing a leading biopharmaceutical industry cluster in Zhejiang Province, focusing on innovative pharmaceuticals, CDMOs, advanced medical devices, and high-end cosmetics, with both domestic and international influence. The overall design concept for the coffee shop needed to embody the connection between the industrial park, technology, and coffee.
